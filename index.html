About The Studio

Built by engineers, run for artists.

Hollowbody Recording Co. started as a single-room tracking space above a guitar repair shop on Fatherland Street. Fourteen years later, we're two full rooms, a mastering suite, and a client list that spans debut EPs to platinum-selling albums — but the philosophy hasn't moved an inch.

We believe a session should feel like a conversation, not a clock. Every room is tuned by ear as much as by measurement, every signal chain is chosen for the song in front of us, and every engineer on staff has spent real years on the other side of the glass as a working musician.

Analog Foundation

A restored Neve 8078 console anchors Studio A, tracked through a mic locker built over two decades.

Modern Workflow

Pro Tools HDX rigs, remote session streaming, and same-week stem delivery for touring artists.

In-House Team

Four full-time engineers and two staff mixers — no rotating freelancers on your project.

Room to Create

A lounge, iso booths for strings and vocals, and a kitchen that's seen more pre-session nerves than any therapist.

Good To Know

Frequently asked questions.

How far in advance should we book?

Day-rate bookings typically need 3-4 weeks of lead time, especially in spring and fall. Single hourly sessions can often be booked within the same week — reach out and we'll check the calendar with you directly.

Do you provide session musicians?

Yes. We keep a network of drummers, string players, horn sections, and keys players we've worked with for years. Let us know what the song needs and we'll put together a lineup and a rate before your session.

Can we mix remotely if we tracked elsewhere?

Absolutely. Send us your session files or stems and we'll handle the rest, with revision rounds conducted over video call and shared reference links. Most remote mixes turn around within 5-7 business days.

What's included in the day rate?

A 10-hour room lockout with a lead engineer, use of the house mic locker and outboard gear, and a rough mix bounce before you leave. Session assistants and additional musicians are quoted separately.

Do you offer payment plans for full albums?

Yes — full album packages are typically split into three milestones: tracking, mixing, and mastering, invoiced as each stage is delivered. We'll lay out the full schedule in your quote.
